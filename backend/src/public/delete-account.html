Delete Your SamSpeak Account

If you wish to delete your SamSpeak account, please follow the instructions below.

Account Deletion Process

  • Send an email to bruste68@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My Account".
  • Include the email address or ID you used to sign up.
  • We will confirm your request and remove your data from our systems within 7 business days.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

  • Your login credentials will be removed.
  • Any learning history and saved preferences will be permanently deleted.
  • This action cannot be undone.
